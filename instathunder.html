Steps to use RDP using Insta Thunder:


  • Download and install Insta Thunder from a trusted source.

  • Open the Insta Thunder application on your device.

  • Log in using your Instagram credentials.

  • Navigate to the RDP section within the app.

  • Enter the RDP server details provided by your service provider.

  • Click on "Connect" to establish the RDP connection.

  • Once connected, you can access your remote desktop through Insta Thunder.

  • To disconnect, simply click on the "Disconnect" button within the app.
